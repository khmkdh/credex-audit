# Unit Economics

## What is a Converted Lead Worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Based on publicly available
information about the AI credits market:

- Average deal size: ~$5,000 in credits purchased per startup
- Credex margin: ~20% (estimated, based on typical reseller margins)
- Revenue per converted customer: ~$1,000
- Repeat purchase rate: ~60% within 6 months (credits get consumed)
- LTV of one customer: ~$1,000 x 1.6 = **$1,600**

- LTV:CAC ratio: $1,600 / $150 = **10.7x** — excellent

## What Conversion Rate Makes This Profitable?

The tool has near-zero marginal cost to run:
- Vercel hosting: $0 (free tier)
- Supabase: $0 (free tier up to 500MB)
- Gemini API: $0 (free tier 1,500 req/day)
- Resend: $0 (free tier 100 emails/day)

At current free tier limits, the tool is profitable at even 1 credit purchase
per month. One $5,000 deal = $1,000 revenue = positive ROI on all time invested.

Break-even is essentially:
- 1 consultation per week → 0.4 purchases/week → $400 revenue/week
- That requires only 10 email captures/week → 40 audit completions/week
- 40 audits/week = ~6 audits/day — trivially achievable after a single HN post

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

Assumptions:
- Average revenue per customer: $1,000 (first purchase)
- Repeat purchase adds another $600 (LTV = $1,600)
- Target: $1,000,000 ARR = $83,333/month
- Required: ~83 new customers/month at $1,000 each

| Month | Focus | Customers/mo | MRR |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1-2 | Organic seeding (HN, Reddit, Discord) | 10 | $10,000 |

| 5-6 | Referral loop activated, repeat purchases | 45 | $45,000 |

## What Must Be True for This to Work

1. The audit tool drives at least 600 completions/month by month 3
2. Consultation booking rate stays above 8%
3. Credex closes at least 35% of consultations
4. Average deal size stays above $3,000
5. At least 50% of customers repurchase within 6 months

These are aggressive but not unrealistic numbers for a B2B tool in a market
where the pain (overspending on AI) is real, measurable, and growing.
